 Open URL: `o`
* Insert: `i`
* Copy the current page url: `yy`

* Copy: `Ctrl+Shift+C`
* Cancel: `Ctrl+C`
* Paste: `Ctrl+Shift+V`
* Open a page or passthrough mode(disable vim bind key): `Ctrl+V`
    * to exit passthrough mode: `Shift + Esc`

* Go back and forth: `H`, `L`
* Open a new tab: `Ctrl + t`
* Cut: `Ctrl + x`
* Close Tab: `Ctrl+W` or `d`
* Select All : `ggVG`

* Switch Tabs: `Alt` + `n`
